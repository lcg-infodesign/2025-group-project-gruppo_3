V

A thorough analysis of the most powerful volcanic events in history, illustrating the deep impact these eruptions have had on human civilizations.

This interactive project analyzes major volcanic events, detailing human consequences from fatalities and injuries to missing persons, destroyed houses, and economic damage. It maps geo-temporal patterns across continents to identify historical hotspots, while classifying volcano types with descriptive illustrations. This framework facilitates a deep comparison of scales and global impact.
